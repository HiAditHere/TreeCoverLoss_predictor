Tree Cover Loss Predictor

Tree Cover Loss Predictor predicts the loss in the Tree Cover based on the release of CO2 and other gases.
I used a dataset from kaggle . It comprised of the following features:

  • Country
  • Tree Cover loss in Hectares
  • Year
  • Release of Co2 and other gases in Mg

    • After some further analysis of the data, I found that the Year and Country did not play any significant role in determining the Tree Cover Loss.
    So I decided to neglect them while traing a Machine Learning Model.

    I have used Flask to deploy it
